  • Makes flood infestors able to heal and revive flood combat forms.
  • Allows hostile mobs to both attack and move, so simply running away should be less viable.
  • Allows flood combat forms to produce infestors, but only one can live at a time.
  • Tones down the effect of slipspade drive overloading. Sadly it's now just a really big explosion, but it shouldn't crash the server anymore.
  • Adds the Cobra, outfitted to function as an anti-armour sniper vehicle.
  • Adds the URF Bull, an old halftrack chassis refitted to function as an APC with a mounted machine gun.
  • Adds the Covenant Wraith, capable of firing deadly balls of plasma in a slow arc over any carefully constructed defenses.
  • Gives T'voans a bit more health and pain resist, for the sake of balancing.
  • Makes vehicles able to carry crates (Pelican, Shadow and Spirit, currently.)
  • Makes BR mags cross-compatible.
  • Lowers overall bomb resists on many armours, again.
  • Adds ship scanning consoles which allow for the targeting of certain areas of a ship and a scan of the ship's overall superstructure strength. Due to lack of mapped targetable areas, no consoles are currently mapped in.
  • Rebalances explosions by adding an optional argument to the explosion proc, which, when used will ensure that within a set radius, an explosion will apply a flat amount of damage to contained mobs.
  • Applies the above changes to rocket launcher explosions and other explosion sources.
  • Makes vehicle have a psuedo-hud element that shows the number of people in a vehicle and their species.
  • Rebalances existing vehicles by nerfing/buffing health, resistances and speed, where appropriate.
  • Adds the Scorpion Main Battle Tank. Currently adminspawn only.
  • Adds the M12 Warthog LRV, Chaingun variant. Currently adminspawn only.
  • Rebalances armor bomb resist values, nerfing/buffing where appropriate.
  • Adds a vehicle repair system. Vehicle repair is a multi-step process. (Must be in Harm mode to do below steps.)
  • Step 1: Examine the components of the vehicle to assess the damage (Inspect Components verb)
  • Step 2: Apply sheets of applicable material (Steel for UNSC + Innie, Nanolaminate for covvies).
  • Step 3: Finalise the repair by using the tools shown in step 1 on the vehicle.
  • Step 3: Congrats, you've repaired a vehicle. Now don't break it again.
  • Destroyed vehicles don't need to be repaired to full to be allowed to move again.
  • Fixes armour repair benches.

XO-11 updated:

  • Adds general-use slipspace drives to the SDV, HA and Thorn.
  • Slipspace transit to a location is a short, multiple step process.
  • Step 1: Activate drive to transport your ship to nullspace after a small (10 second) delay.
  • Step 2: Wait for cooldown to expire (Currently a 1 min, 15 second wait)
  • Step 3: Activate slipspace drive again to enter co-ordinates for exit location after a small (10 second) delay.
  • Step 4: Arrive at location, repeat process once drive has fully recharged (5 minute delay).
  • Increases the size of the overmap. 100 -> 125
